About Me

I'm Ananda S.F.I. I'm currently attending at SMK Muhammadiyah Adiwerna and an Animation Major. My Main Skill is Illustration, from Sketches to Fully Rendered Artwork with Clip Studio Paint.

My Hobbies are Drawing, Reading, Pacing Around My Room For An Indefinite Amount Of Time, and Sleeping. I'm a Cat Lover first and foremost, and a Human Being second.

Gallery

"Apple Of My Eye." Artwork of my own Original Characters, Agathe--a vampire--holding up a bitten apple with Eleanor--her prey--reflected in it, hidden behind blood. Used Clip Studio Paint for the Artwork.

"The Sum Of My Parts." Artwork of my own Original Character, Eleanor, who's standing in front of a wall full of portraits. She's looking down with an inscrutable gaze and armed with a crossbow. The portraits behind Eleanor depict significant memories of her life. Several of them are worn down, ripped off, notched with an arrow, and obscured in the dark. Used Clip Studio Paint for the Artwork.

"Here Comes The Rising Dawn." Artwork of my own Original Character, Eleanor, who's tilting her head up serenely with one of her hand clutching her arm. Behind her is a progress of a dove flying upwards even after one of its wings caught on fire, grazed by the rising sun, only to--of course--inevitably fall down. Her expression is one of surrender. Used Clip Studio Paint for the Artwork.

"Into The Unknown." Fanart of the Cartoon 'Over The Garden Wall' with the characters Wirt, Greg, and Beatrice venturing deeper into the woods with The Beast observing them, hidden behind the trees, a lone glowing eye the only thing visible. Used Clip Studio Paint for the Artwork.

"In A Hurry." Fanart of the Podcast 'Malevolent' with the character Arthur Lester in a hurry to go somewhere, his hair askew and his messenger bag dragging down his coat, papers spilling out of it. He looks annoyed as he tries to adjust his coat and right his bag. Used Clip Studio Paint for the Artwork.

"Snowy Morning." Fanart of the Podcast 'Malevolent' with a monochromatic picture that shows the characters Arthur Lester and John Doe taking their time in the winter morning, basking in the warmth of the sun. Arthur has a lighter in his hand near John's face, lighting his cigarette. Smoke trails upwards from it. The picture is scribbled with their name and date in cursive. Used Clip Studio Paint for the Artwork.

"Ready To Strike." Fanart of the Show 'The Mighty Nein' with the characters Nott the Brave climbing up Caleb Widogast's back, readying their attack. A strong flame burns brightly at Caleb's hand and an arrow is drawn in Nott's crossbow, the 'Dancing Lights' cantrip flowing around them. The borders are tapestry-like and a Lumina Beetle can be seen in top left corner. Used Clip Studio Paint for the Artwork.
